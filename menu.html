Real Ice Cream.
Nothing Else.
We use real ice cream — no powder, no mix, no shortcuts. Our shakes are made the old-fashioned way: hand-spun in a metal cup, thick enough to need a spoon. Milkshakes have been on the In-N-Out menu since 1975, and the recipe has never changed.
